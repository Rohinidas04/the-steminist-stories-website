About Us

We share the stories of women in STEM fields and the passion of young STEMinists

The STEMinist Stories is dedicated to empowering and inspiring young girls to choose STEM fields by sharing the success stories of women in STEM. By sharing the inspiring tales of women who have shaped society, the STEMinist Stories hopes to reach millions of girls nationwide and decrease the gender gap. We also have a blog where young STEMinists can share the stories of how they became passionate in fields of STEM. If you'd like to be featured in an interview or blog or have any questions, feel free to contact us!
